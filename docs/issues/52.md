# Issue #52 ‚Äî Cover-boundary flip should animate the cover leaf only

## Symptom

In spread mode with `coverMode='single'` (cover-alone), the transition across
the cover boundary animated the **entire surface** (the blank half + cover
half on the way out, or the full first spread on the way back). The single-leaf
realism delivered for non-cover spreads in #45 was not reused here, so the
cover transition looked tonally different from every other page turn.

## Resolution

Introduced a dedicated leaf component, `OutgoingCoverLeaf`, that handles the
two cover-boundary transitions in spread mode with `coverMode='single'`:

- **Forward (`0 ‚Üí 1`)**: the cover leaf peels off the right half. The
  underlying static is the first spread (`pages[1] | pages[2]`); a blank
  phantom on the left half preserves the resting cover-alone look until the
  leaf back face (which carries `pages[1]`) arrives past 90¬∞.
- **Backward (`1 ‚Üí 0`)**: the same leaf settles back onto the right half.
  Because the leaf reuses the forward-direction preset frames with `initial`
  and `final` swapped, the DOM position and pivot are identical between
  directions ‚Äî the cover never DOM-jumps between sides. A `pages[2]` phantom
  on the right keeps the prior spread look intact until the leaf front face
  (the cover) lands at 0¬∞.

The leaf wrapper carries `transform-style: preserve-3d` and no
`overflow: hidden`, matching the #49 fix so the back face composes correctly
in 3D space. Rounded corners are applied to the face `<img>`s directly when
the rounded-corners option is on.

### Render branching

`PageFlip` now picks the leaf flavor in this priority order:

1. `shouldUseCoverLeaf` ‚Äî only fires for `mode='spread'`, `coverMode='single'`,
   and a 0‚Üî1 transition. Renders `OutgoingCoverLeaf`.
2. `shouldUseSpreadLeaf` ‚Äî non-cover spread transitions. Renders
   `OutgoingSpreadLeaf` (introduced in #45).
3. Fallback `OutgoingLayer` ‚Äî single-mode and any remaining edge case.

## Regression Coverage

- `src/components/reader/PageFlip.test.tsx`:
  - forward `0 ‚Üí 1` mounts a half-width cover leaf on the right with the
    cover on the front face and `pages[1]` on the back face, and a blank
    phantom on the left
  - backward `1 ‚Üí 0` keeps the leaf on the right half and the phantom holds
    `pages[2]` on the right
  - cover leaf wrapper preserves 3D rendering (no `overflow-hidden`)
  - backward cover leaf reverses the rotation ‚Äî initial transform is the
    spread final frame, final is the resting frame
  - deeper navigation (spread ‚Üí spread, no cover involved) still uses the
    regular spread leaf
- `e2e/reader-cover-mode.spec.ts`:
  - forward cover-boundary flip animates a cover-only leaf (right half, w-1/2)
  - backward cover-boundary flip uses the same right-half leaf and a
    `pages[2]` phantom on the right
