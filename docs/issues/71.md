# Issue #71 — v2 realistic classic flip

- **Status**: Ready for review
- **Branch**: `feat/71-v2-classic-flip`

## Problem

v2 Reader was a stub. Classic flip in v1 felt flat (pure `rotateY`) rather than turning real paper.

## Approach

1. **Fork v1 reader stack into `src/v2/`** — data, hooks (v2-namespaced storage keys), lib, reader components, Reader page. No `@v1/` imports.
2. **Classic preset rewrite** — `buildPaperClassicTransform()` with perspective(2200px), subtle `rotateX(-2.5deg)`, `translateZ` lift, `EASE_PAPER` easing, progressive `boxShadow`.
3. **Spine overlay** — spread-mode gutter intensifies while `outgoing` is active (`data-testid="page-flip-spine"`).
4. **v2 Gallery** — book cards linking to `/v2/book/:id`.

## Trade-offs

- Full fork duplicates ~40 files until v2 diverges further; required by plan-2 isolation.
- CSS-only paper bend (no mesh); WebGL deferred to M2.2d.
- Flicker (#72) and spine pop at `final` (#73) intentionally left for follow-up issues.
